Ten Tenths provides Technical Due Diligence, and Coaching for technology leaders.

Technical Due Diligence

For investors or ahead of acquisition

Ten Tenths provides technical due diligence targeted at early- to mid-stage startups (up to 50 technical employees, up to £5m investments, up to £50m valuations).

Engagements range from “kicking the tyres” conversations to in-depth investigations lasting 5 days or more. The resulting report will normally cover the full range of software development activities, but the approach can be extensively customised to concentrate on areas of particular concern.

Coaching

For CTOs and other technology leaders

Technical leadership within a startup can be lonely. As the only member of the senior team with a technology background, it can be difficult to find someone to “bounce ideas off,” discuss problems, and consider personal development.

Ten Tenths provides highly customisable coaching for CTOs and other technology leaders ranging from one-off ad-hoc conversations to more structured programmes with regular contact and an agreed development plan.

Technical Due Diligence

For investors or ahead of acquisition

Ten Tenths provides technical due diligence targeted at early- to mid-stage startups (1–50 employees, up to £5m investments, up to £50m valuations).

Engagements vary from hour-long “kicking the tyres” conversations to in-depth investigations lasting 5 days or more. The focus is typically on the software engineering process and culture, but can be extensively customised to focus on areas of particular concern.

Coaching

For CTOs and other technology leaders

Technical leader within a startup can be a lonely position. As the only member of the senior management team with a technical background, it can be difficult to find someone to “bounce ideas off”, discuss problems, and consider personal development. This can be particularly true for new or inexperienced CTOs.

Ten Tenths provides highly customisable coaching for CTOs and other technology leaders ranging from one-off ad-hoc conversations to more structured programmes with regular contact and an agreed development plan.
